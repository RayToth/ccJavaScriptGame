Play!

Instructions

Hello traveler! Your job is to defend the base from the evil monsters. In order to do that
you have to manage your towers reasonably.

Rules

- You have 3 health and each monster deals 1 damage if they reach your base.
- If you lose all your health you will lose.
- If you can withstand all the waves you win.

Towers

- Can shoot one place further to any direction.
- The first tower costs 100 gold.
- You can upgrade your tower by double clicking on your tower
and choose "Upgrade tower" function.

Enemies

- You get gold if you kill them.
- Each wave becomes stronger.

Good luck and have fun!
